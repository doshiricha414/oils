Suggested protocols:

Different folks will find different oils blend best with their bodies and their preferences in odors.  Experiment and find the best of the suggestions above for individual needs.

·    The simplest technique is to apply the oils directly. 

·    Or premix the oils with a carrier oil for periodic use.  A 1 - 5% dilution would be typical.  That is 3 - 15 drops of essential oil to 1 tablespoon of carrier oil

·    For easy application premix as a lotion.  For directions for making salves and lotions click here.
