In order to achieve & maintain a full spectrum of great health - the first thing we should rethink is the doctrine of everything in moderation.

If you knew your morning cup of coffee contained a small dose of Arsenic - would you still drink it faithfully every day? Probably not. Because you know that it's not the small dose that ends up killing you - it's the cumulative effect. Now, what if you believed that a packet of Splenda or double pump of Sugar Free Syrup would have the same effect over the long haul - would you reconsider it? For some, it's not as easy to answer that question as you'd think.

We live in a pick-your-poison society. When everything has the potential to sicken or kill you - it's easy to default into apathy. Everybody knows Chemical Sugars are terrible for you. But when you hear that over half the Sugar consumed in the US is from GMO Sugar Beets that have been heavily doused with Pesticides - the decision becomes more of a brain teaser.  Artificial Sweetener = Neurotoxin & Metabolic Disrupter. "Real" Sugar = Insulin Resistance (which leads to Diabetes). Easy to see why many people just choose the lesser of 2 evils and then plant their heads firmly into the sand. 

But once you or a loved one gets sick, it's hard to defend that rationale. The best time to shake yourself loose from a state of denial is BEFORE something terrible or irreversible happens. We know this from personal experience.  That is why we've chosen to share our hard-earned knowledge with you. Our approach to Wellness Care is almost rudimentary - stop unknowingly poisoning yourself on a daily basis. You may not be able to eliminate every toxin but you can drastically reduce your exposure. If you know what to look for. We want to give you a hand with that.

Toxic Free Zone will help you educate yourself on the worst everyday chemical additives and offer alternatives to replace the products you use that contain them. Got Sick...Now What? will focus on what you can to do to help your body recover from more serious illnesses.

Since Food is such an important part of the puzzle - we've dedicated an entire site to it. Cleaner Plate is all about Clean Eating - focusing on minimally processed Organic Produce, Grains & Meats with Dairy-Free, Gluten Free & Vegan options. Good Food makes you Feel Good - and Food as Medicine is a much easier pill to swallow. And it offers far better side effects - like glowing skin and increased energy, for starters!
