Massage blend combines the therapeutic benefits certified therapeutic grade essential oils, well-known for their properties that relax muscles and soothe joints, promote circulation and help to calm and soothe target areas.  It's a perfectly relaxing blend, that adds many important benefits to various massage techniques.

Massage blend combines the esssential oils of basil, grapefruit, cypress, marjoram, peppermint and lavender. Is used in a proprietary method for applying essential oils to produce a profound whole-body wellness experience.

The scent of Massage blend can be described as clean, fresh, minty and powdery.

Uses of the Massage blend:

  • Apply to neck and shoulders, to promote muscle relaxation and soothe the joints; or use with a carrier oil for massage.
  • Give your loved one a hand massage, using the Massage Hand Technique.
  • Add to hot bath water after a busy, stressful day.

Primary Benefits of the Massage blend:

  • Relaxes muscles and soothes joints
  • Promotes circulation
  • Helps to calm and sooth target areas

How to use the Massage blend:

Topical use: Apply one to two drops to desired area.  Dilution is not required, but you may dilute with fractionated coconut oil, to minimize any skin sensitivity.

Cautions: Possible skin sensitivity.  Keep out of reach of children.  If you are pregnant, nursing or under a doctor's care, consult your physician.  Avoid contact with eyes, inner ears and sensitive areas.  Avoid sunlight or UV rays for up to 12 hours after applying product.
