Focus Blend is a combination of carefully selected essential oils for their ability to enhance focus and support healthy thought processes.  Supporting the efforts of those with difficulty paying attention and staying on task, these essential oils help sustain focus.

The essential oils in Focus Blend work together, supporting your efforts to remain calm and focused on a desired task.

10 mL roll-on bottle.

Focus Blend has a very pleasing, distinctive aroma that is musky, spicy, earthy.  Formulated from the certified  therapeutic  grade essential oils of West Indian sandalwood (Amyris balsamifera), patchouli (Pogostemon cablin), frankincense (Boswellia carterii), lime (Citrus aurantifolia), ylang ylang (Cananga odorata), Hawaiian sandalwood (Santalum paniculatum) and Roman chamomile (Anthemis nobilis), Focus Blend is very calming and soothing.

Primary benefits of Focus Blend

  • Enhances and sustains focus
  • Supports efforts of those who have difficulty paying attention and staying on task
  • Promotes healthy thought processes

Uses for Focus Blend

  • Apply to the temples and back of neck, to enhance concentration.
  • Apply to wrists or back of hands and inhale deeply, to promote focus and clarity.
  • Use at work, to stay on task, or when feeling tired mid-afternoon.
  • Dispense on a clay pendant and place on a desk or in other key areas in a classroom.

How to use

Focus Blend can be used aromatically or topically. 

  • Topical use: Apply one to two drops to desired area.  It is gentle enough to use neat, however, dilution with carrier oil will help to minimize any possible skin sensitivity.  See additional precautions below.

Cautions: Possible skin sensitivity.  Keep out of reach of children.  If you are pregnant, nursing, or under a doctor's care, consult your physician.  Avoid contact with eyes, inner ears, and sensitive areas.
