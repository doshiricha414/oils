Pneumonia

Pneumonia can vary from mild to life threatening.  For serious conditions seek professional medical attention. Listed below are protocols others have found successful.  For some it may require the combination of all the procedures. Remember consistency is a must for success.

 

Oils & Blends: Respiratory Blend, Frankincense, Lavender, Lemon, Melaleuca,  Protective Blend, Oregano, Peppermint

Essential oils based products: Massage Blend Technique, Protective Blend hand wipes and foaming cleanser

Also consider: Basil, Cinnamon, Cypress, Eucalyptus, Marjoram, Rosemary, Thyme

Suggested protocols:

Antibacterial/antiviral blend

Blend in a 00 capsule and take 3 times per day or apply on bottoms of the feet:

·  4 drops Protective Blend

·  3 drops  Oregano

·  3 drops Frankincense

·  fill with FCO

 

Chest and inhale

Put Respiratory Blend on the chest several times per day and cup and inhale from your hands after every application.  You could augment the inhalation therapy by diffusing or making a steam bath with Respiratory Blend by placing a towel over your head and breathing the fumes deeply as the oils volatize (keep your eyes closed).   Also consider the T Shirt technique.

 

Gargle and Oil Pull

Gargle and spit Oregano and Lemon with one tablespoon of water or carrier oil hourly. Use a shot glass and be careful not to allow the oils to touch lips. Also hourly oil pull with FCO and Protective Blend.

 

Lots of water with oils

Drink lots of liquid (water) with drops of Lemon and Peppermint in it.

 

Enema Blend

Two retention enemas of the blend listed below.  One at night before bed time, a second (set the alarm) 2 hours prior to getting up in the morning.  This will allow at least one fullsleep cycle before the enema. (the reason for this uncomfortable protocol is that this is one of the more effective ways to get oils deep into the lungs)

·  2 drops Protective Blend

·  2 drops Oregano

·  2 drops Rosemary

·  2 drops Frankincense

·  2 drops Peppermint

·  Fill the remainder of a 00 capsule with FCO and insert appropriately or add the oils and approximately 25 drops of carrier oil to the enema syringe.

Blends may also be added to a capsule and used as a suppository.

 

Massage Blend Technique or Back Massage

At least once daily, do a back treatment using Grounding Blend, Thyme, Oregano, Basil, Cypress, Marjoram and Protective Blend.  Applying the oils one at a time, and following up with a healthy massage and then apply heated towels to the back for about 5 minutes or until the towels cool to body temperature, reheat and apply again.
