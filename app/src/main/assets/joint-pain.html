Joint Pain

Joint pain comes from a variety of sources including overuse, injury, damage from past injury and disease. It can range from mild irritation to debilitating pain and can last from a few hours to a chronic condition lasting years. The most common factor in joint pain is inflammation and often, even if the root cause cannot be addressed, limiting the inflammation can be a great benefit. The proper balance of rest and exercise is also a consideration and varies greatly from condition to condition.
