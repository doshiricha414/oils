Overview: Essential oils are renowned for helping one feel more energetic and productive. There may be underlying reasons for fatigue, but even if fatigue is a symptom of a larger problem, essential oils can offer relief. Other causes of fatigue may be emotional like depression, hormonal imbalances, and stress. These cause may be obvious, if it is not, it might be important to see a physician.
