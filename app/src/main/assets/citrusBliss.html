Invigorating blend merges the powerful benefits of popular citrus essential oils into a harmonious blend, with a sweet, citrusy aroma.  Its benefits extend beyond elevating mood and reducing stress; it has natural compounds with potent cleansing and purifying properties, both for the air and surfaces.  Mixed with water, Invigorating Blend is a perfect solution for cleaning countertops and other surfaces, while producing a fresh, sweet scent.

This distinctively unique and harmonious blend, combines the powerful essences of wild orange, lemon, grapefruit, mandarin, bergamot, tangerine and clementine, with just a hint of vanilla.

How to use

The Invigorating blend essential oil blend can be used topically or diffused into the air.  It is gentle enough to be used neat, although it can be diluted with fractionated coconut oil, if desired. 

  • Diffusion: Use three to four drops in the diffuser of your choice.
  • Topical use: Apply one to two drops to desired area.  Dilute with fractionated coconut oil, to minimize any skin sensitivity.  See additional precautions below.

Cautions: Possible skin sensitivity.  Keep out of reach of children.  If you are pregnant, nursing, or under a doctor's care, consult your physician.  Avoid contact with eyes, inner ears and sensitive areas.  As with all citrus oils, this blend should not be applied to skin that will be exposed to direct sunlight or UV rays for up to 12 hours after applying product.
