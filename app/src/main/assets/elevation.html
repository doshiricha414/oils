It is probably an experience common to all of us: You just feel kind of blah and sluggish, for no particular reason.  At times like that, Joyful Blend is the perfect blend for you.  In those moments, when you need to be revitalized, the uplifting and euphoric floral scents of this exciting blend of certified therapeutic grade essential oils can elevate your mood and increase your energy.

You will find in Joyful Blend a unique blend, that provides you with an invigorating combination of essential oils.  Joyful Blend promotes a positive mood and energized mind and body; flawlessly blended, to create an immediate and dramatic effect.

Joyful Blend is an exquisite blend of lavandin (Lavandula hybrida), lavender (Lavandula angustifolia), Hawaiian sandalwood (Santalum paniculatum), tangerine (Citrus reticulata), melissa (Melissa officinalis), ylang ylang (Cananga odorata), elemi (Canarium luzonicum), osmanthus (Osmanthus fragrans) and lemon myrtle (Backhousia citriodora), with a floral, sweet, citrus scent.

Primary benefits of Joyful Blend

  • It elevates your mood and increases your vitality
  • Its aroma is energizing and refreshing
  • It helps to lessen sad and anxious feelings
  • It promotes a positive mood
  • It energives you, mind and body

Uses for Joyful Blend

  • Diffuse Joyful Blend, to promote feelings of self-worth, while also helping to lessen sadness and anxious feelings.
  • Diffuse Joyful Blend in the morning, before waking kids, to help start the day with a positive, uplifted mood.
  • Rub Joyful Blend oil over your heart, temples or wrists, for an almost immediate mood booster.
  • After a long, stressful day, take a bath with a few drops of Joyful Blend, to help relieve anxious feelings and restore the mind and body.

How to use

Joyful Blend is intended for aromatic or topical use.

  • Diffusion: Use three to four drops, in the diffuser of your choice.
  • Topical use: Apply one to two drops to desired area.  While Joyful Blend is gentle enough to use neat, dilution with carrier oil will help to minimize any possible skin sensitivity.  See additional precautions below.

Cautions: Possible skin sensitivity.  Keep out of reach of children.  If you are pregnant, nursing, or under a doctor's care, consult your physician.  Avoid contact with eyes, inner ears, and sensitive areas.  Avoid sunlight or UV rays for up to 12 hours after applying product.
