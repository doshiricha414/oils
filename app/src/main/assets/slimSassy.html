Metabolic Blend is  designed to promote healthy metabolism, stimulate the endocrine system, help to purify and cleanse the body, and help to manage hunger cravings between meals, while promoting a positive mood.  Together, these oils provide powerful support for your weight loss plan.

Metabolic Blend contains grapefruit (Citrus paradisis) and lemon (Citrus limon) oils, both of which have a high limonene content, to help purify and cleanse the body, while peppermint (Mentha piperita), ginger (Zingiber officinale) and cinnamon (Cinnamomum zeylanicum) provide a stimulating and positive effect on the endocrine system. 

When taken with a healthy eating and exercise plan, Metabolic Blend, with its warm, spicy, herbal aroma, can help with your weight loss plan.  Just add 8 drops of  Metabolic Blend to 16 oz of water, and drink between your healthy meals throughout the day, to help manage hunger, calm your stomach and lift your mood. Metabolic Blend is 100% pure essential oils, does not contain diuretics or stimulants, and has zero calories.

  • Add a few drops to Metabolic Blend TrimShake or V Shake.

How to use

Metabolic Blend can be used aromatically, topically or internally.

  • Diffusion: Use three to four drops in the diffuser of your choice.
  • Internal use: Dilute one drop in 4 fl oz of liquid. Metabolic Blend is also available in softgel capsules.  You may also add 8 drops to 16 fl oz of water (regular-sized water bottle), and drink between your healthy meals throughout the day.  Be sure not to use a plastic bottle.
  • Topical use: Apply one to two drops to desired area.  While dilution might not be required for most individuals, dilution, with carrier oil, recommended for those with skin sensitivity.  See additional precautions below.

Cautions: Possible skin sensitivity.  Keep out of reach of children.  If you are pregnant, nursing, or under a doctor's care, consult your physician.  Avoid contact with eyes, inner ears, and sensitive areas.  Avoid sunlight or UV rays for up to 12 hours after applying product.
