How to use

Respiratory blend can be used aromatically or topically.  It should be diluted for use on sensitive skin.

  • Diffusion: Use three to four drops in the diffuser of your choice.
  • Topical use: Apply one to two drops to desired area.  Dilute with fractionated coconut oil, to minimize any skin sensitivity.  If diluted Respiratory Blend can be used even the smallest of children.

    See additional precautions below.

Cautions: Possible skin sensitivity.  Keep out of reach of children.  If you are pregnant, nursing or under a doctor's care, consult your physician.  Avoid contact with eyes, inner ears and sensitive areas.  Avoid sunlight or UV rays for up to 12 hours after applying product.
