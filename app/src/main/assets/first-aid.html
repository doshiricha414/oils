First Aid

Essential Oils are exceptional at first-aid.

Our top 3 favorites to always have on hand:

Lavender works wonders for insect stings & bites & burns of all kinds. It is also a topical pain reliever.

The antiseptic virtues of Melaleuca make it great for cleansing wounds & preventing infection.

Peppermint works equally well for nausea as it does for fever reduction
