Poison Ivy

Urushiol is a clear liquid that is within the sap of poison ivy, poison oak, and the less common poison sumac.  When this sap comes in contact with the exposed skin of most people is will cause an itching rash.  The scientific name for the rash is Toxicodendron dermatitis. 

Oils & Blends:  Birch, Eucalyptus, Frankincense, Lavender, Myrrh, Protective Blend, Peppermint

Also consider: Roman Chamomile, Rose
