Rosacea

Rosacea is an adult chronic skin condition wherein there are red areas of inflammation on cheeks, nose, forehead and/or chin.  The redness occurs as the blood vessels in the area expand sometimes accompanied by bumps similar to pimples. Those that suffer from it notice that it comes and goes and seems to be triggered by things like outside exposure, stress, exercise, and alcohol.  People with fair skin seem to be more susceptible.  If left untreated rosacea will continue to worsen.
