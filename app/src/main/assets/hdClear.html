Topical Blend is a unique blend of black cumin (Nigella Sativa) seed oil, as well as the essential oils of Ho (Cinnamomum camphora linalooliferum) wood, melaleuca (M. alternifolia or tea tree), eucalyptus (E. globulus), geranium (Pelargonium graveolens) and litsea (Litsea cubeba).

Used as a spot treatment, or as an over-all application, Topical Blend: Topical Blend promotes a clean and healthy complexion, with essential oils renowned for their skin-benefiting properties.

10 mL roll-on bottle.

A must-have blend for families, Topical Blend can be applied topically, to promote clear, smooth skin, to soothe skin irritations and to assist with cell renewal.  It helps to keep the skin clean and well-hydrated.

For aromatic and topical use.
