Bronchitis

Bronchitis is a temporary inflammation of air passages going to the lungs.  It can be caused by a bacterial infections, a viral infections, or a chronic (long term) condition from smoking or other irritants.  Since a continued infection can lead to pneumonia it is best to treat bronchitis early and aggressively.

 

Oils & Blends: Respiratory Blend, Clove, Eucalyptus, Frankincense, Lavender, Protective Blend, Peppermint, Rosemary

Also consider: Basil, Cypress, Lemon, Marjoram, Thyme

Suggested Protocols:

Apply Breath topically to chest (Add Eucalyptus or Rosemary if needed) for coughing symptoms.

Protective Blend (supplement with Basil, Frankincense or Lemon) topically on the throat area as an Antibiotic.

For more severe Bronchitis take Protective Blend (supplement with Lemon & Oregano) in a capsule 2 to 4 times daily.

Also diffuse Respiratory Blend (or Eucalyptus or Rosemary) or use a cup and inhale.
