Hangover

Oils & Blends:  Lavender, Lemon, Rosemary

Also consider: Grapefruit, Sandalwood

Suggested protocols:

Some suggest:

2 - 3 drops each of Lavender, Lemon and Rosemary in a warm bath for soaking and relaxing.

Or make a blend of:

•  1 - 2 drops Grapefruit

•  1 - 2 drops Lavender

•  1 - 2 drops Lemon

•  1 - 2 drops Rosemary

•  1 - 2 drops Sandalwood

•  blended with 1 tablespoon of Fractionated Coconut Oil

Massage blend on neck and back and consider bottoms of feet.
