Cellulite

Also consider: Cypress, Frankincense, Geranium, Sandalwood

Suggested protocols:

Cellulite - Consider the full Metabolic Blend Protocol as described under weight loss.  With special emphasis on topical:

•  Helichrysum oil and Metabolic Blend blend -  Topically apply Helichrysum essential oil then layer with Metabolic Blend Blend to locations with cellulite and other fatty accumulations.

Flabby Skin - Consider the full Metabolic Blend Protocol as described under weight loss. With special emphasis on topical, tone and conditioning:

•  Grapefruit oil and Metabolic Blend blend -  Topically apply Grapefruit essential oil then layer with Metabolic Blend Blend to locations with excess fat accumulations.

•  Tone and Condition  -  Consider the dōTERRA Skin Care products Anti-Aging Moisturizer, Tightening Serum, and Invigorating Scrub to tone and condition the skin.  Self made blends of individual oils can be used as well or may augment these products.  Recognize in extreme cases it will take extended periods of time and consistency for progress.
