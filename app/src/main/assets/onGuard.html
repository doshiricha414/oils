Protective Blend is formulated to support healthy immune function and protect you from environmental threats.  It offers a fragrant, natural and effective alternative for keeping your family and home healthy.

Protective Blend with its unique aroma, is one of our most versatile blends.  It can be use aromatically, topically, or as a dietary supplement.  It may also be used to purify the atmosphere; and it is also safe to use on counter tops, as a non-toxic way to cleanse surfaces.

Protective Blend protects against environmental and seasonal threats, with essential oils known for their support of healthy immune function.  It's versatility makes it ideal for use in many types of products.  Its warm, spicy, camphoraceous, woody aroma offers you a fragrant, natural and effective way to protect against environmental threats, clean surfaces and fill your house with its energizing and uplifting aroma.

Protective Blend can be taken internally, on a daily basis, to maintain healthy immune function (see the Protective Blend  + Softgels and Protecting Throat Drops), as well as used on surfaces throughout the home, as non-toxic cleaner.  When diffused, Protective Blend helps purify the air, supports healthy respiratory function, and can be very energizing and uplifting.  Applied topically, Protective Blend provides cleansing and purifying benefits, while promoting healthy circulation.

The unique aroma of  Protective Blend comes from a combination of the therapeutic grade oils of  wild orange (Citrus sinensis), clove (Eugenia caryophyllata), cinnamon (Cinnamomum zeylanicum), Eucalyptus radiata and rosemary (Rosmarinus officinalis).

  • Supports healthy immune function
  • Protects against environmental threats

Uses of  Protective Blend

  • Gargle with a few drops  as a healthy mouth rinse or for an irritated throat.
  • Add to a dishwashing sponge or cloth, to purify and eliminate odors.
  • Soak sliced apples in water, and a few drops for a healthy, immune-boosting snack.
  • Combine a few drops  with fractionated coconut oil, for a natural hand cleanser.  (See also the Protective Blend foaming hand wash.)
  • Use on stainless-steel surfaces, to eliminate tough, caked-on spills.

How to use

Protective Blend can be used aromatically, topically or internally.

  • Aromatically: Use three to four drops in the diffuser of your choice.  You may also use direct inhalation.  Just put a few drop in the palm of your hands, rub your hands together and cup them over your face and take several deep breaths.
  • Internal use: Dilute one drop in 4 fl oz of liquid, or use the Protective Blend+ SoftGels or  Protecting Throat Drops.
  • Topical use: Apply one to two drops to desired area.  Dilute with fractionated coconut oil, to minimize any skin sensitivity.  See additional precautions below.

Cautions: Possible skin sensitivity.  Keep out of reach of children.  If you are pregnant, nursing, or under a doctor's care, consult your physician.  Avoid contact with eyes, inner ears and sensitive areas.  Avoid sunlight or UV rays for up to 12 hours after applying product.
