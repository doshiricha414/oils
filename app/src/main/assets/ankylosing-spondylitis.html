"Spondyloarthropathies are a group of diseases which affect the vertebral column and Ankylosing spondylitis is one among them. It is one of the rheumatic disease, which is a chronic, inflammatory that causes arthritis of the spine and sacroiliac joint - the joint at the base of the spine where the spinal column joins the pelvis.

"Ankylosis" means the fusion of vertebrae to each other. The vertebrae fuse together because of the chronic inflammation and irritation of the spinal joints. Ankylosis is a debilitating condition that can cause decreased range of movement in the spine and loss of spinal mobility and function, thereby, severely impact a person's quality of life.
